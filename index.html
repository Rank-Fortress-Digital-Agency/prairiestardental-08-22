WHAT IS DENTAL BONDING, AND HOW DOES IT WORK?

Dental bonding is a type of dental procedure that can improve the appearance of your smile. It involves using a unique resin material applied to the teeth and then hardened with a high-intensity light. Bonding can be used to correct a variety of dental problems, including:

  • Gaps between the teeth
  • Chipped or cracked teeth
  • Discolored teeth
  • Misshapen teeth

Bonding is usually a relatively quick and easy procedure, and it can be completed in just one visit to the dentist. The bonding results are generally relatively natural-looking, and the bonded materials can last many years with proper care. It is important to note that bonding is not as strong as other dental procedures, such as veneers or crowns. However, it can be a good option for people who want to improve the appearance of their smile without undergoing extensive dental work.
